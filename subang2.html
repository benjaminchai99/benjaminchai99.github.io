HELP Subang 2

Welcome to HELP Subang 2 campus

OVERVIEW


HELP University’s (HU) Subang 2 campus is part of a 23 acre site located at the door step of the upcoming Kwasa Damansara, which is undergoing a massive commercial transformation, supported by substantial infrastructure investments such as the upcoming MRT Sungai Buloh station. Located along Persiaran Cakerawala, Taman Subang Delima, Shah Alam, HU’s Phase 2A spans 3.3 acres and will house the psychology and business faculties, accommodating up to 2,500 students. Being an education institution of high repute, HU is expected to serve as a development catalyst for the immediate Subang Bestari neighbourhood and the upcoming Kwasa Damansara.

Located at the door step of the upcoming Kwasa Damansara, the campus is situated nearby the upcoming MRT Sungai Buloh station. Its 3.3 acres campus can accommodate up to 2,500 students and is fully equipped with modern facilities.

THEME



Using tropical rainforest as the principle theme for the Subang 2 campus, the form and flow of the university expresses a sophisticated kind of harmony with the elements, mirroring what is experienced in our tropical rainforests. Selected fruit trees and lifestyle water features will be part of its landscape.

ARCHITECTURE



One of the key statements of this campus is its enthusiastic dedication to going green and being energy efficient. Passive green design features such as open corridors, cross ventilation, rain water harvesting, energy efficient lighting, use of recycled materials, sunlight shading and harvesting of natural day light will be incorporated throughout the campus.
